Interviewer: Last season, SU struggled with 3-point shooting. What’s the plan moving forward?
Coach: We’re focusing on perimeter drills and improving shot consistency. Our players will get more game-like reps.

Interviewer: The team’s rebounding was one of its strengths. How do you plan to keep that edge?
Coach: Conditioning and physicality are part of our identity. We’ll continue to emphasize both.

Interviewer: What are your main goals for the next season?
Coach: Better perimeter accuracy, stronger endurance, and building on our defensive intensity.
